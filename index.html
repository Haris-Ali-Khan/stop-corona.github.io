What is COVID19?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people, and those with underlying medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer are more likely to develop serious illness.

210 Countries and Territories around the world have reported a total of 2M confirmed cases of the coronavirus COVID-19 that originated from Wuhan, China, and a death toll of 100K deaths.

#FightCoronaVirus


Don't be afraid and fight. Everyone should keep a healthy lifestyle at home. Maintain a healthy diet, sleep, stay active, and make social contact with loved ones through the phone or internet. Children need extra love and attention from adults during difficult times. Keep to regular routines and schedules as much as possible

Five Steps to kicking

out the Corona Virus

Wash your hands

It starts with your hand so frequently wash your hands with soap and water or an alcohol based solution.

Cover your mouth

Avoid touching your face, particularly your eyes, nose or mouth to prevent the virus from entering your body.

Cover with elbow

 Cover your nose and mouth with bent elbow or tissue when you sneeze or cough. Dispose the tissue immediately and wash hands.

Avoid close contact

Stay at least one meter distance from others, Avoid social interaction.  

What you feel

If you feel unwell stay Home & follow the instruction by your local health authorities.
